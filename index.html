Privacy Policy for LinkedIn RTL Aligner

Effective Date: September 23, 2024

1. Introduction

LinkedIn RTL Aligner (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is a Chrome extension designed to enhance your experience on LinkedIn by aligning posts and comments in right-to-left (RTL) languages.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users of LinkedIn RTL Aligner. Our extension modifies the display of content on LinkedIn solely based on user interactions without accessing or retaining any user information.

3. Use of Data

Since we do not collect any data, we do not use it for any purpose.
